why pwa?

The web won. Your app should be on it.

A PWA is an app that lives on the web. Tap "Save to Home Screen" once and it sits next to Instagram — same icon, same launch, same feel. Better than the App Store. Here's why.

The old way
📱
Native iOS / Android app
  • Apple takes 15-30% of every transaction. Google takes 15-30%. Forever.
  • Submit, wait days, hope they don't reject you. Repeat for every update.
  • $5K-$80K to build. Two codebases (iOS & Android). Two engineering bills.
  • $99/year Apple developer fee. $25 Google fee. Compliance overhead.
  • Customer must search the App Store, find you, download — a 30-second mountain.
  • Apple can de-list your app on a whim. You don't own the relationship.
VS
The app. way
a.
PWA — Progressive Web App
  • 0% platform fees. Stripe directly. You keep every dollar minus card fees.
  • Instant updates. Push a fix at 9am, customers see it at 9:01am. No reviews.
  • One codebase, every device. iPhone, Android, iPad, desktop — identical.
  • Works offline. Local storage built-in. Your app keeps working with no signal.
  • Install in 2 seconds. Tap your link, tap "Save to Home Screen," done.
  • You own it. No platform between you and your customers. Direct, forever.

Starbucks, Twitter (X), Pinterest, Uber, BMW, and Spotify all run as PWAs. You can too.
