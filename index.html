A Progressive Web App is a website that installs and behaves like a native iOS or Android app. Customers tap "Add to Home Screen" once, and your app launches full-screen with no browser bar. It works offline, sends push notifications, and looks identical to a downloaded app — without going through the App Store or Play Store.
Native apps cost $15K-$80K to build, take 3-6 months, and Apple takes 30%. PWAs cover 95% of the same use cases at 1% of the cost. If you eventually need a native app, the PWA codebase ports easily — we can do that as an upgrade.
Yes — we can wrap your PWA in a native shell and submit it to the App Store and Google Play for an additional fee. Most clients skip this. Customers find your app via your domain or QR code, install in one tap, and you keep 100% of revenue.
Every tier includes revisions during build. After launch, edits are billed at $50 / hour, or you can subscribe to white-glove support ($75/month) for unlimited minor changes and priority response.

Yes. Every tier includes hosting, SSL, a global CDN, and 99.9% uptime on our infrastructure. No hidden fees. The only thing that costs extra is bringing your own domain ($10/month add-on) and any backend services from the add-ons list above.
